# commends for data check 
Check All Students
Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:8000/students/" -Method Get

Check Today's Attendance
Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:8000/attendance/today" -Method Get

Check in Table Format (Nicely Formatted)

# Students table
Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:8000/students/" -Method Get | Format-Table id, name, roll, department

# Attendance table
Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:8000/attendance/today" -Method Get | Format-Table id, student_id, status, session_date, marked_at

Quick One-liner — Present or Absent?
Invoke-RestMethod -Uri "http://localhost:8000/attendance/today" -Method Get | Select-Object student_id, status, marked_at | Format-Table
